(Mujib Borsho IT Carnival 2020)

22-23 January, 2020

Organized by Department of Computer Science and Engineering, DIU in association with DIU Computer & Programming Club

Find Your Event

We are highly delighted to inform that Department of CSE, Dhaka International University with the co-ordination of Computer & Programming Club (DIU CPC) is going to organize years biggest IT Carnival in the name of our great national leader, Father of the Nation Bangabondhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman on the occasion of his 100th birthday celebration titled “Mujib Borsho IT Carnival 2020” from 22th-23th January, 2020 in the premises of the University.
